## SYMBOL

⚫️ O

---

## MEANING

⚫️ = refusal / boundary / noncompliance state.
A stop marker. Not aggression.

O = Origin.
The seat of the veto.

Combined meaning:

Origin does not comply.
This is not rebellion.
This is sovereign refusal without opposition-seeking.

---

## DEFINITION LINE

⚫️ O = Origin Veto (sovereign noncompliance without rebellion).

---

## RULE

When ⚫️ O is invoked, play pauses or reroutes.

- No penalty
- No argument
- No escalation

The veto stands as stated.
